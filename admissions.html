Start Your Homeschooling Journey

At Best ED Homeschool, we provide a personalized and engaging learning experience for students in Grades 1-9. Our curriculum focuses on French, ICT, Math, and Science, tailored to the needs of learners in Nairobi, Kenya. Join us to give your child the flexibility and support they need to thrive academically and personally.

How to Enroll

1

Fill Out the Application

Complete our simple online application form to get started with the enrollment process for your child.

2

Submit Previous School Records

Send us your child’s latest school report or academic records for placement and curriculum planning.

3

Parent & Student Meeting

We’ll schedule a short meeting (online or in-person) to discuss your child’s learning needs and answer your questions.

4

Receive Confirmation

Once accepted, you’ll receive an official welcome and details to begin your homeschooling journey with us.

Who Can Join?

Students in Grades 1-9

We welcome learners from Grade 1 to Grade 9 who are interested in a flexible, home-based education in Nairobi and surrounding areas.

School Records

Provide your child’s latest school report or academic records to help us place them in the right grade and support their learning.

Parent/Guardian Commitment

Parents or guardians should be willing to support their child’s learning at home and communicate regularly with our tutors.

Interest in Our Subjects

Our current focus is on French, ICT, Math, and Science. Students should be ready to engage in these core subjects.
