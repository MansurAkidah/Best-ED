Our Story

Homeschooling for a Brighter Future

Best ED Homeschool was founded to provide accessible, high-quality education to students in Nairobi, Kenya from Grade 1 to Grade 9. Our program is designed to support families seeking a flexible, personalized approach to learning, with a focus on core subjects: French, ICT, Math, and Sciences.

Our Beginning

Started with one passionate educator offering personalized homeschooling services in Nairobi, bringing expertise in core subjects to a small group of students.

Teacher Network

Built a team of qualified teachers specializing in French, ICT, Math, and Sciences, creating a collaborative environment for sharing best practices in homeschooling education.

Expanding Our Reach

Connecting with educators across Nairobi, establishing a network of certified teachers ready to provide quality homeschooling support.

Today's Vision

Aiming to operate on a nationwide level, matching qualified teachers with homeschooling families, ensuring quality education is accessible throughout Kenya.

Our Mission

To empower families through personalized homeschooling that cultivates a love for learning and critical thinking. We provide tailored resources and guidance, ensuring each child thrives in a nurturing environment that respects their unique learning style. Together, we inspire lifelong learners.

Our Vision

To be the leading catalyst for educational excellence in homeschooling, nurturing every child's potential. We create an innovative learning ecosystem that embraces diversity and fosters independent thinking. Through expert guidance and community support, we prepare students to excel as empowered global citizens.
